Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

ConcertSync ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use ConcertSync for macOS.

1. Information We Collect

ConcertSync is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal information:

  • Audio Data: Audio is processed entirely on your local device. We never record, store, or transmit audio data.
  • Hue Bridge Data: Connection to your Philips Hue Bridge occurs over your local network only. Bridge IP and authentication tokens are stored locally on your Mac in the system Keychain.
  • Analytics (Optional): If you opt in, we collect anonymous usage statistics (app opens, feature usage) via TelemetryDeck. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash reports help us improve stability. These contain no personal data.

2. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely to provide and improve ConcertSync. We use analytics to understand which features are popular and crash reports to fix bugs.

3. Data Storage and Security

All sensitive data (Hue Bridge credentials, preferences) is stored locally on your Mac using the macOS Keychain and app sandbox. We do not operate servers that store your data.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Philips Hue: ConcertSync communicates with your Hue Bridge on your local network. We do not access your Philips/Signify cloud account.
  • Apple: If you subscribe, Apple handles all payment processing. We never see your payment details.
  • TelemetryDeck: Optional privacy-first analytics. No cookies, no personal data. You can opt out in Settings.

5. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You have the right to: access your data, request deletion of your data, opt out of analytics, and data portability. Since we store virtually no data about you, these rights are easily fulfilled. Contact us at support@concertsync.dev for any privacy requests.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

ConcertSync is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns: support@concertsync.dev
