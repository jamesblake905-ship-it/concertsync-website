FAQ

Everything you need to know

Do I need a Philips Hue Bridge?▼
Yes, ConcertSync requires a Philips Hue Bridge v2 or later for secure local network communication with your lights.
Is my audio data sent to servers?▼
No. All audio processing happens locally on your Mac. We never record, store, or transmit your audio.
Works with Spotify/Apple Music?▼
Absolutely. Choose system audio as your source. Works with any app that plays audio on your Mac.
What macOS versions?▼
macOS 13.0 (Ventura) or later. Works on Apple Silicon and Intel Macs.
