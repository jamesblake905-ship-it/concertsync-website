Features

Every Feature a Concert Needs

Professional-grade light synchronization powered by real-time audio analysis and the Hue Entertainment DTLS streaming protocol.

Real-Time Audio Sync

Captures system audio directly and analyzes frequency bands in real time. Lights respond to bass, mids, and highs independently for rich layered effects.

DTLS Streaming at 50Hz

Direct encrypted streaming to your Hue bridge over DTLS. Color updates hit your lights 50 times per second with virtually zero perceptible latency.

Gradient Light Support

Full support for Signe floor lamps and gradient lightstrips. Each segment receives interpolated color data for smooth flowing gradient effects.

Concert Mode Presets

Switch between EDM, Rock, Jazz, Ambient, and Custom presets. Each mode tunes the frequency response curves and color palettes for the genre.

Visual Scene Editor

Map your lights in 3D space and assign frequency bands, color ranges, and intensity curves. Drag and drop to design your perfect light show.

Up to 10 Lights

Control up to 10 entertainment lights simultaneously. Mix standard bulbs, Signe lamps, and gradient strips in a single synchronized configuration.

How It Works

Three Steps to a Light Show

ConcertSync handles the complex bridge communication so you can focus on the music.

Connect Your Bridge

Press the button on your Hue bridge and ConcertSync auto-discovers it on your network. Credentials are stored securely in your macOS Keychain.

Arrange Your Lights

Select which lights to include, position them in the scene editor, and choose a concert mode preset or build your own custom mapping.

Play Music

Hit start and play any music from any app. ConcertSync captures system audio and streams color data directly to your lights over encrypted DTLS.

ConcertSync works with any Philips Hue light that supports the Entertainment API. This includes standard color bulbs (E26/E27), Hue Play bars, Signe floor and table lamps, gradient lightstrips, and Go portable lights. A Hue Bridge (square model, v2+) is required.
